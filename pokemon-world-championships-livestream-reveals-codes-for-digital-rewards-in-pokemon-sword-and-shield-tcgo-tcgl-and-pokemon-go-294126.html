Pokmon World Championships livestream reveals codes for digital rewards in Pokmon Sword and Shie

September 2024 · 2 minute read
As announced together with the streaming schedule last week, limited-time distributions of digital rewards for a variety of Pokémon games are currently being distributed in celebration of the 2022 Pokémon World Championships. These include a distribution of the Victory Pokémon, Victini, for Pokémon Sword and Pokémon Shield, a Path to the Peak card for the Pokémon Trading Card Game Online or Pokémon Trading Card Game Live, and a 2022 Worlds Tee avatar item for Pokémon GO.

Victini distribution
Players can redeem Victini using the code「W0RLD22V1CT0RY」, which was revealed during the Twitch livestream of the event. This code can only be redeemed until August 22nd, at 11:59pm (UTC).

Pokémon: Victini
OT: Victory
ID: 220818
Level: 50
Poké Ball: Cherish Ball
Ability: Victory Star
Nature: Brave
Held Item: Starf Berry
Moves: V-create, Zen Headbutt, Work Up, and Flame Charge

How to receive the Victini via Mystery Gift:

  • Launch Pokémon Sword or Pokémon Shield from the Nintendo Switch HOME Menu.
  • Press the X button during gameplay to access the in-game menu.
  • Select MYSTERY GIFT.
  • Select Get a Mystery Gift.
  • Select Get with Code/Password.
  • Enter code「W0RLD22V1CT0RY」and select OK.
  • Select Victory Pokémon Victini Gift.
  • Be sure to save your game after receiving your gift.

Path to the Peak digital card distribution
Pokémon Trading Card Game Online or Pokémon Trading Card Game Live players can receive a Path to the Peak Secret Rare card from the Astral Radiance expansion using the code「Worlds22London」. As of writing, this code cannot be redeemed in app, and must be redeemed through the official redemption website.

This code can only be redeemed until August 28th, at 11 p.m. PDT.

2022 Worlds Tee distribution
Players of Pokémon GO can receive the 2022 Worlds Tee avatar item using the code「WRGUZRVKRR2M3」. The code will be valid through August 22nd, at 11:59 p.m. UTC.
