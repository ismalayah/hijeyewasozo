Who is Gina’s Husband?

New Yorker, Gina, is a new addition to The Real Housewives of Orange County.

Gina certainly wasn’t warmly welcomed into the group as the rest of the ladies seem to judge her life choices.

The mum-of-three explained on the show that she and her husband Matt live separately as he moved away for work, leaving her in Orange County with their children, Nicholas, Sienna and Luca.

Matt never appeared on RHOC due to his employer not wanting him to be on the show.

Gina told the other ladies that Matt would visit when he could, however, she explained that the time apart had made her question whether she was really in a loving marriage.

What Happened Between Gina and Matt?

The 34-year-old OC newbie caused mass confusion when she explained to the other housewives that she and Matt, although planning on divorce, were still sleeping together.

An awkward dinner at Vicky Gunvalson’s house resulted in Gina’s morals being questioned.

Gina goes through some difficult times during season 13 but it looks like she and Emily have made friends for life since meeting on RHOC.

She says on the show that although she and Matt are still attracted to one another, she feels they got married and had children young and that it could be the wrong decision to stay married.

View Instagram Post

Are They Still Divorcing?

Yes! Although its taking some time since Gina first mentioned the divorce, it’s still going ahead.

Gina shared an update on her divorce proceedings during an interview with Bravo’s ‘The Daily Dish’ in October 2018 and said: “We’re actually sitting down with our attorneys, like all together, on November 2 to just basically finish up everything…It’s complicated getting divorced, and there’s a lot of paperwork.”

It looks like Gina and Matt are keeping things as ‘normal’ as possible for their children, staying united as a family but parting ways as a couple.
