Who is Madalyn Horcher and what is her ethnicity?

Madalyn Horcher was born in the USA, on 22 September 1995 – her zodiac sign is Virgo and she holds American nationality. She is an actress who gained recognition after she portrayed Chloe Solano in the crime mystery mini-series “Gracepoint”, starring David Tennant, Anna Gunn and Virginia Kull, and which is about the police investigating the murder of a young boy – the series was very successful, and won one award while it was nominated for two others.

Early life and education

Madalyn was raised alongside her two younger sisters, Mira and Madison, both of whom are also actresses – Miya is known for her roles in “Fallout 4” in 2015 and “Killer in Suburbia” in 2020, while Madison is popular for starring in “Mail Order Monster” in 2018 and “Think Like a Dog” in 2020.

Madalyn became interested in acting while attending elementary school, where she was a member of the school’s drama club, and appeared in nearly all the plays performed there. She continued practicing acting upon enrolling at a local high school, and after finishing her first year in 2010, made her debut TV series appearance, guest starring as Beyonca in the episode “Puppy Love” of the family series “My Parents, My Sister & Me”, which actually aired only 10 episodes before it was cancelled.

Madalyn matriculated from high school in 2013, and then chose not to attend college but to focus on her acting career.

Roles in TV series

After making her debut TV series appearance, Madalyn was invited to appear in single episodes of several series such as the medical mystery “House”, the comedy series “Fred: The Show”, and the family comedy series “Good Luck Charlie”.

In 2012, she was invited to guest star as Jacinta Flores in the episode “Wild Flowers” of the critically acclaimed crime mystery series “CSI: Crime Scene Investigation”, starring Laurence Fishburne, George Eads and Marg Helgenberger, and which is about a team of forensic evidence investigators who are working on cases in Las Vegas, Nevada.

Madalyn’s following role came two years later, when she portrayed Nikki in a single episode of the action comedy series “Kickin’ It”, and again two years later, she guest starred as Jaydin in the episode “Second Year” of the drama series “Code Black”. In 2016 as well, Madalyn appeared in two series – “NCIS” and “Sweet/Vicious” – while 2017 saw her guest star as Kelsey in the action crime series “Hawaii Five-0”, and as Abby Carpenter in two episodes of the romantic series “The Night Shift”.

In 2018, Madalyn appeared in a single episode of the action crime series “Chicago P.D.”, and then went on to star as Abigail Garcia in the horror science fiction series “Origin”, also starring Natalia Tena, Tom Felton and Sen Mitsuji, and which is about a group of people fighting mysterious creatures on their spaceship.

Two of Madalyn’s latest roles in TV series have been in the comedy crime series “The Rookie”, and the action adventure series “MacGyver” both shot in 2019.

Roles in movies

Madalyn made her debut film appearance in 2012, when she portrayed Tara Vazquez in the action film “Rogue”, starring Angela Bassett and Julian McMahon, and which follows a professional assassin who accepts a job from a teenage girl whose father was murdered. A year later, Madalyn played the supporting character Daisy Garcia in the short comedy film “The Garcias Have Landed”.

In 2015 she appeared in the horror thriller film “Curve” which received mixed reviews, and in the same year portrayed Dani in the thriller film “A Teacher’s Obsession”, and Julia Adams in the drama movie “The Advocate”.

The following year saw her play the supporting character Sgt. Leach in the action thriller film “Jack Reacher: Never Go Back” which starred Tom Cruise, and Madalyn’s latest role in a movie has been in 2017, when she played Angelina Torres in the comedy film “Charlie Foxtrot”, but which failed to attract much attention.

Love life and husband

Madalyn has been keeping the details of her love life to herself, but there are rumors circulating the internet that she is currently in a relationship with Glenroy Brown, who is an American real estate investor.

According to the rumors, the two met at a red carpet event in 2018 when they were introduced by a mutual friend, and started dating only days later, while it’s also believed that Glenroy has recently become Madalyn’s husband. Neither of the two have shared pictures of themselves together onto their social media networks, but have allegedly been seen spending time together on multiple occasions.

It is also believed that Madalyn was in a long-term relationship with her high school sweetheart, and that they split upon matriculation after two years of going out. As of August 2020, she is apparently still dating Glenroy, hasn’t married and doesn’t have children.

Hobbies and interests

Summer is Madalyn’s favorite season, as she enjoys laying at the beach and sun tanning as well as swimming in the ocean.

She is a lover of animals and has a pet dog which can often be seen featured in the pictures she uploads onto her Instagram account. Madalyn likes to travel, and her job as an actress has taken her all around the US, while she’s also been to several European countries, such as Spain, England and France.

She is a fan of tattoos and has one inked on her left ribs.

In her spare time and mostly late at night, Madalyn likes to watch movies featuring some of her favorite actors and actresses, who are Johnny Depp, Robert Downey Jr. and Julia Roberts, and some of the movies she enjoys watching the most are “Edward Scissorhands”, “Iron Man” and “Notting Hill”.

Age and net worth

Madalyn’s age is 24. She has long brown hair and brown eyes, is 5ft 6ins (1.67m) tall and weighs around 125lbs (56kgs).

As of August 2020, her net worth has been estimated at over $200,000.
